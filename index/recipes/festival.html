Jamaican Festival

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups (250 g) Plain flour
  • ¼ Cup (39.75 g) Fine yellow
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • 4 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • ⅔ Cup (157.73 ml) Unsweetened milk (or water)
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Add the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt, and sugar to a mixing bowl and combine these dry ingredients well.
  2. Gradually pour the milk into the dry ingredients and knead until you form a soft dough. (A soft dough but not paste-like).
  3. Add enough oil to a deep frying pan and heat on medium to low fire.
  4. Divide the dough into smaller portions (about 10-12 pieces) and roll it in the palm of your hands to form it into an oval-like shape.
  5. Place the dough one at a time into the heated oil. Constantly turn dumplings so that it cooks on all sides.
  6. Once the dumplings get golden brown, remove from the oil.
